No matter the occasion you can celebrate at our place!

ABOUT US

A stunning combination of contemporary American fare with Italian influences, Mariner’s Bistro in Edgewater, New Jersey creates a dining experience that is deliciously familiar yet refined and innovative. With elegant indoor and outdoor dining, genuine hospitality and exquisite food Mariner’s Bistro boasts it all.
